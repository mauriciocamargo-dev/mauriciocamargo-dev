## Current Focus: Xat CRM

I am currently building **Xat CRM**, a WhatsApp-first CRM designed for teams that manage conversations, candidates, customers, support, onboarding, and operational workflows through messaging.

Xat CRM is being built from real operational needs, not assumptions.

It includes:

- Multi-inbox WhatsApp conversations
- Contact and candidate matching
- Internal notes
- Historical conversation context
- Public API and webhooks
- WhatsApp connector lifecycle and reconnection hardening
- Real-time conversation updates
- Automation-ready architecture
- Self-hosted deployment architecture

The goal is to create an open-core CRM platform for businesses that want more control, flexibility, and ownership over their communication workflows.
